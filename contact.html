Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer personalised travel itineraries?

Yes! Our travel experts can help you craft a personalised itinerary tailored to your interests, budget, and travel dates. Just reach out via the contact form and select "Itinerary Planning".

Are your destination recommendations free?

All of our destination guides, tips, and recommendations are completely free to access on TravelVista. We're supported by affiliate partnerships and display advertising, never by sponsored placements.

Can I contribute a destination or travel story?

We love hearing from fellow travellers! Send us your story via the contact form with the subject "Partnership & Collaboration" and our editorial team will review it.

How often is the travel content updated?

Our team reviews and updates destination content on a rolling basis, with major reviews every six months. We also update immediately when significant travel advisories are issued.
